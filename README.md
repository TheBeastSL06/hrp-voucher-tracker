# HRP Voucher Tracker

A Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to provide transparency and real-time updates on the status of monthly vouchers submitted to the Account Branch of the **Horowpothana Divisional Secretariat**.

🔗 **[Access the Live App Here](https://thebeastsl06.github.io/hrp-voucher-tracker/)**

## 🎯 Purpose
The primary goal of this application is to allow any officer to easily check their voucher's progress (e.g., Staff Travel, GN Travel, Overtime, Bills) without needing to visit or contact the Account Branch physically.

## ✨ Features
* **Real-Time Dashboard:** Overview of total vouchers and their current stages (Pending FA, Pending VL/Dep, Pending Payment, Paid).
* **Category Management:** Vouchers are grouped logically for easy tracking.
* **Status Badges:** Clear visual indicators for each step of the approval and payment process.
* **Role-Based Workflow:** Dedicated views and actions for Financial Assistants (FA), Vote Ledger Officers (VL), Deposit Ledger Officers, and Super Admins.
* **Bilingual UI:** Easily switch between English and Sinhala (`සිං`).
* **PWA Support:** Installable on PC, Android, and iOS directly from the browser.

## 📱 Installation (PWA Guide)
You can install this app directly on your device for quick access:
* **PC (Chrome/Edge):** Open the link and click the "Install" icon (a screen with a downward arrow) in the address bar.
* **Android (Chrome):** Open the link, tap the three-dot menu in the top right, and select **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**.
* **iOS (Safari):** Open the link, tap the **"Share"** icon at the bottom, scroll down, and select **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## 📊 Understanding Voucher Statuses
When you click on a category card, you will see the status of individual vouchers indicated by badges:
* ⚪ **Pending:** Not yet received by the Account Branch.
* 🔵 **✓ Received:** Currently being processed at that specific stage (FA / VL / Deposit).
* 🟡 **⚠ Alloc. Not Enough:** The financial allocation is insufficient to process the voucher.
* 🟡 **⚠ Alloc. Not Received:** The financial allocation for this voucher has not been received.
* 🟢 **✓ Paid:** Payment has been finalized.

## 💡 Staff Guidelines
1. **Check the App First:** Please refrain from making frequent physical or phone inquiries. Use the app to check the real-time status.
2. **Verify the Period:** Ensure you have selected the correct Year and Month at the top of the dashboard.
3. **Follow Up on "Pending" Status:** If your voucher continuously shows as "Pending", it means it hasn't reached the Account Branch. Coordinate with your subject officer to expedite the submission.
